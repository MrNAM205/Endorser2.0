
IRM 4.10.7.2.8 (09-30-2022)
Notice of Deficiency

1. A notice of deficiency is a legal determination of a tax liability. It is a taxpayer's "ticket to the Tax Court." [...] The notice must describe the basis for, and identify the amounts (if any) of, the tax due, interest, additional amounts, additions to the tax, and assessable penalties included in the notice. An erroneous description... shall not invalidate the notice.

2. The notice of deficiency must be sent to the taxpayer by certified or registered mail.
